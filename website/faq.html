Who is behind Aperio GNU/Linux?

Biel Polastrini.

When does a new version gets released?

Whenever Debian GNU/Linux gets updated.

Which architectures does Aperio support?

x86_64.

Why another GNU/Linux distribution?

  1. I wanted a big project to work on.
  2. I've always wanted to have a GNU/Linux distribution of my own, I tried it before but got overwhelmed by package management. That's one of the reasons I chose to base Aperio GNU/Linux on Debian.
  3. This one is tailored for me. I got tired of repeating the same installation proccess everytime I needed to install Debian on a new computer, so, Aperio handles everything.
